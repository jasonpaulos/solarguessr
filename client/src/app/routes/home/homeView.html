SolarGuessr

Imagine GeoGuessr, but for the whole solar system

Play Now

Want to get more familiar with our home in the universe?

SolarGuessr places you on a celestial body in our solar system, and you have to figure out exactly where you are.

Look around by clicking and dragging the screen, then guess where you are when you're ready.

Your guess will be given two ratings: the system score and planet score.

  • The system score is based on how close you are the actual planetary system
  • If you choose the right planet, the planet score is based on how close you are to the actual location on that planet
